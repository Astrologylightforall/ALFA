The living room (Baithak) is where family members assemble and guests are received. Its energy dictates the overall mood of the entire household.

Here are 5 core Vastu guidelines to implement:

### 1. The Entrance (North/East)
The best direction for a living room entrance is the North, East, or North-East. This direction welcomes light and prosperity.

### 2. Seating Arrangements
The head of the family should ideally sit in the South-West corner, facing North or East, which gives them stability and authority.

### 3. Electronic Placement
Place TVs, air conditioners, and other heavy electronic equipment in the **South-East** corner of the room, as this is the Agnidev (fire) direction.

### 4. Furniture Clearance
Avoid heavy furniture that blocks the center of the room (the **Brahmasthan**). Keep it open and light.

### 5. Color Palette
Use light colors like off-white, beige, light yellow, or light green. Avoid dark, depressing tones like heavy black or grey on all walls.
