The transit of the Sun into Pisces (Meena Sankranti) marks a beautiful period for introspection and spiritual growth. Pisces is a water sign ruled by Jupiter, making this a harmonious blend of solar energy and higher wisdom.

### Key Effects
1. **Heightened Intuition**: You may find your gut feelings are more accurate during this period.
2. **Emotional Sensitivity**: It is important to guard your energy and practice groundedness.
3. **Charity & Giving**: A highly auspicious time for donating or volunteering.

### Zodiac Forecasts
- **Aries**: Focus on rest and meditation. 12th house activation.
- **Taurus**: Gains through social networks and elder siblings.
- **Gemini**: Excellent for career expansion and new responsibilities.
